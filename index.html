A little about me.

Hello, my name is Alissa Lorberau and I reside in a small town just outside of Corpus Christi, TX with my family and I. My journey into tech first started when I had my twins. I just never wanted to leave their side and so I researched ways to work from home. I started with basic coding and slowly but surely I gained confidence in my abilities. I am now a front end developer.

Through tech I have satisfated my hunger for new horizons and a way to be home more with my family. Always have I been fascinated by the world of technology and the endless possibilities it holds. I have a strong passion for learning and growing, and I am always looking for new challenges to take on. I am excited to share my skills and experiences with you and to see where this journey takes me. When I am not bringing the world great code, you can find me cozying up with a good book or spending time with my family.
