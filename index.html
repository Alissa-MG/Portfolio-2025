Hello, my name is Alissa Lorberau and I reside in a small town just outside of Corpus Christi, TX with my family. My journey into tech first started when I had my twins. Even though, I had a rewarding career in the medical field, I didn't enjoy being away from my children for long hours, so I looked into challenging careers that allowed me to work from home and that's how I found my love for coding.
I started with basic coding and slowly but surely I gained confidence in my abilities. I am now a front-end developer. Through tech I have satisfied my hunger for new horizons and a way to spend more time with my family. I am excited to share my skills and experiences with you and to see where this journey takes me.
